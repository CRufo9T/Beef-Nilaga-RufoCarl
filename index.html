Beef Nilaga

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef brisket(cubed)
  • 1 big potato(cubed
  • 1 bok choy or pechay
  • 1 large onion(chopped
  • 2 medium size carrots(chopped crosswise length 2 inches)
  • 1 tbsp whole pepper corn
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Step 1. In a large pot, combine the beef and water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Skim off any scum or impurities that rise to the surface to ensure a clear broth.
  2. Step 2. Add the onion, garlic (if using), and beef bouillon cube (optional). Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender. If using a pressure cooker, cook for about 30-40 minutes.
  3. Step 3. Once the beef is tender, add the potatoes and carrots to the pot. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until they are almost tender.
  4. Step 4. Add the cabbage wedges and green beans (or pechay) to the soup. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked but still crisp.
  5. Step 5. Season the broth with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  6. Step 6. Ladle the beef, vegetables, and broth into bowls. Serve hot with steamed white rice and a side of fish sauce with calamansi or lemon for dipping.
